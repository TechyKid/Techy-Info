How to Turn HTML CSS and Javascript Into a Android App



And Trust Me It is Easy



Have you just wanted to test your Android app on a real device well, it is crazy simple









Step #1


Open up Your preferred web browser





Step #2



go to Adobe Phonegap It may or may not ask you to sign in with your Adobe account



Step #3



Asumming that you are signed in lets continue



Click on the apps tab on top of the screen





Then open up the File Explorer and go to the folder where the files are downloaded and put them into a zip folder.



Click The Upload button on Phonegap




Click Ready To Build and click on the android tab to download the APK



You Can edit the app name and icon using a config.xml file
